HALAL CERTIFIED

OMGYRO is all about serving food you can feel good about. All of our meat is 100% halal and prepared in line with Islamic dietary guidelines, which means no pork or pork by-products (including gelatin or shortenings) and careful attention to how each animal is handled. Paired with fresh, locally sourced ingredients and bold, house-made flavors, our halal dishes let you enjoy every bite with confidence, knowing exactly what’s on your plate.
