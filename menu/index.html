Plates

    finely ground chickpeas, onions, parsley, garlic, and spices; deep fried into semi circles

  • Combo

    a la carte plate with falafel, hummus, and baba ganouj; substitute any vegetarian item at no additional cost; add meat for an additional charge (lamb, chicken, beef)

    thinly sliced lamb made with signature spices and cooked slowly on a stand up rotisserie

    marinated cubes of chicken breast; skewered and grilled
